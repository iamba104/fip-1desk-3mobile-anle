Welcome Information

Welcome to Couples Resort, Soyers Lake. Haliburton's Only 5 STAR Luxury Resort. Fine Luxury while still being Rustic with Wilderness. A Boutique Luxury Resort with Junior suite rooms and cottages that are each designed uniquely, just for the 2 of you, varying between Opulent Old European to Natural Hailburton Style. Come and experience very spacious accommodations, each and all, with Jacuzzi's, Wood Burning Fireplaces, Hot Tubs and so much more.

"For over 50 years we have been committed to helping couples stay connected. 100% Canadian Owned, By the LeBlanc Family since 1958."

Chateaus & Suites

Chateau Room

Our Chateau Rooms are elegantly decorated with old world European furnishings and decor. Each room features a king size bed, wood burning fireplace, whirlpool bath tub, private outdoor hot tub on the deck or balcony, European shower with multiple shower heads, and a spacious lakeside view. Enjoy modern amenities such as a 50 inch television, Keurig coffee maker, and free WIFI.

Villas

Romantic Villa

Our Romantic Villas offer a luxurious retreat with stunning lake views. Each villa includes a king size bed, wood burning fireplace, whirlpool bath tub, private outdoor hot tub on the deck, and a European shower with multiple shower heads. The villas are equipped with modern amenities such as a 50 inch television, Keurig coffee maker, and complimentary WIFI.
